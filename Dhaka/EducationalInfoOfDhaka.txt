	University of Dhaka
			The University of Dhaka, is the oldest university in modern Bangladesh. Established in 1921 during the British Raj, 
			it has made significant contributions to the modern history of Bangladesh. Wikipedia
			Address: Nilkhet Rd,Dhaka 1000
			Chancellor: Abdul Hamid
			Founder: Syed Nawab Ali Chowdhury
			Founded: July 1921
			Vice-chancellor: AAMS Arefin Siddique
			Undergraduate tuition and fees: Domestic tuition: 3,600 BDT (2012), International tuition: 1,200 USD (2012)

			Phone: 02-7110415
			Founded: 2005
			Total enrollment: 27,000 (2010)

	Jahangirnagar University
			Jahangirnagar University is a public university in Bangladesh, based in Savar Upazila, Dhaka. It is the only 
			fully residential university in Bangladesh. Wikipedia
			Address: Savar
			Phone: 01624-850013
			Founded: 1970
			Chancellor: Abdul Hamid
			Undergraduates: 10,983
			Vice-chancellor: Farzana Islam
	Daffodil International University
			Daffodil International University, is a co-educational private university located in Dhanmondi, Dhaka, Bangladesh. 
			It was established on 24 January 2002 under the Private University Act, 1992. Wikipedia
			Address: 102 Mirpur Rd, Dhaka 1207
			Phone: 02-9138234
			Chairperson: Md. Sabur Khan
			Total enrollment: 8,000 (Nov 5, 2012)
			Founded: 2002
			Vice-chancellor: Lutfar Rahman
			Colors: Blue, Green
