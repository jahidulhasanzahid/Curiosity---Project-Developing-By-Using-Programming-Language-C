	1.Shahjalal University of Science and Technology
				Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST) was established in 1987. The only university of its kind at that time, 
				it started it's journey on the 13th of February 1991 with only three departments: Physics, Chemistry and Economics, 13 teachers 
				and 205 students. It has now expanded to 7 schools, 26 departments and a number of institutes and centres. The number of teachers 
				has grown to 487 and the students to 9262. Besides, the University has 8 affiliated medical colleges under the School of Medical 
				Sciences with 2744 students.
